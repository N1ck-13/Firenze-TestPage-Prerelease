Collections

Everything a well-dressed week requires.

FIRENZE isn't online shopping yet — it's a small, curated selection you're welcome to browse in person. Here's a sense of what's usually on the rail. Ask about a piece and we'll confirm it's in stock and in your size before you make the trip.

Firenze — Formalwear
Suits · Tuxedos · Occasion Wear

Formalwear

Weddings, work and evenings that call for a proper suit. Structured shoulders, a clean drape through the leg, and a Florentine sensibility about proportion — cut to look considered, not costume-y.
